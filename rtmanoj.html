Shinjuku

Shinjuku is a vibrant district known for its skyscrapers, shopping, and entertainment.

Asakusa

Asakusa is famous for the historic Senso-ji Temple and traditional Japanese atmosphere.

Harajuku

Harajuku is renowned for its fashion-forward streets and quirky pop culture.

Ginza

Ginza is a high-end shopping district, home to luxury brands, department stores, and fine dining.

Akihabara

Akihabara is an electronics hub and a haven for anime, manga, and gaming enthusiasts.
